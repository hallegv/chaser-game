Swishy Fishy

Can you help the fishy survive the dangerous oceans?!
Click your mouse to enlist help from your magical mermaid friend who distracts the sharks with her ravenous beauty and Plankton from Spongebob who gives you health!
Beware, their help comes with a cost-- 5 new sharks!
If you find yourself in an emergency, press the space bar to eliminate a shark!
Let’s see how long you can last in the deep waters, good luck on this adventure!
